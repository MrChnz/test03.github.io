"Tapsilog"

A tasty and filling way to start the day, "tapsilog" is a popular Filipino breakfast dish that consists of marinated beef (tapa), garlic fried rice (sinangag), and a sunny-side-up egg (itlog).

CHICKEN ADOBO

Tender chicken marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, and spices gives chicken adobo, a savory-sweet Filipino dish, a deep umami flavor. It's a balanced flavor option that is high in protein and low in fat, making it a healthy option.

Lumpiang Shanghai

"Lumpiang Shanghai" is a type of Filipino spring roll that combines crunchy texture and savory ingredients. It is filled with spiced ground beef or pig, wrapped in thin pastry wrappers, then deep-fried until crispy.
